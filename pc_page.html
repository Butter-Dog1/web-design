About my computer

I built my computer at the start of 2021, I had never built a computer before so this was a first.
When I first put together a parts list a picked cheaper parts thinking I wouldn't be getting anything
better but as I saved more and more I kept thinking "if I just save a little bit more I can get this
but better." Eventually I settled for a higher end gaming pc and got this.
